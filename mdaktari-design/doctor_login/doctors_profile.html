Professional Statement

***Please note, our office has a 24 hour cancellation policy. If you need to cancel or reschedule within 24 hours of your appointment, please contact the office and speak with a member of our reception team.

Rich Sarmiento is a registered physical therapist in the state of New York for the past 10 years. He currently owns and practices at NYPT Health & Rehab PLLC that specializes on sports medicine and orthopedic conditions. He has a dynamic approach in rehab which has an emphasis on promotion, prevention and personalized care.

His clinical exercise program encompasses a solid approach in health care from improving function to the implementation of strength and conditioning protocols. All treatments are one on one care from 30 to 45 minutes.
